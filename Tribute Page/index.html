The Norwegian Forest Cat

a particularly fluffy kind of cat

Some fun facts:
  • Known as the Skogkatt in its native Norway. The term skogkatt literally means “forest cat.”
  • The Norwegian Forest Cat has an insulated, waterproof double coat that was designed to withstand the Scandinavian winters of its origin.
  • They were not exported from Norway until the late 1970s, and the first pair arrived in the United States in Novemberof 1979
